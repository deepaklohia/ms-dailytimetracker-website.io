how to use

Daily time tracker for your daily task tracking .


Go to "Home" tab and click "Daily Time Tracker" icon to load..


1.Click on reset to Clear (first time)

2.Click Start to Start

3.Click Stop to Stop

4.Click lap to record , Start+ Stop

Troubleshoot

  • Add-in not loading? :

    1.Clear browsing history and try again.

    2. Ensure that JavaScript is enabled in browser.


    Add-On showing old version on desktop? :

    Clear the Office cache and try again.
